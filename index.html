ehiScript!

“ehiScript!” is an iOS app written in Swift developed for actors and aspiring ones who wants a way to learn and revise a script without needing another person who helps them. This app provide a couple features: insert new script simply by uploading a PDF file into the app, use of speech synthesis to read the lines of the other characters and use of speech recognition to notify the user of his errors during review.
